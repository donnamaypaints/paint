About the Artist

Donna May Boyes

Canadian artist Donna May Boyes has been creating captivating works of art for over two decades. With a focus on the interplay between color and emotion, her work bridges the gap between abstract expression and representational art.

Inspired by the natural landscapes of Canada and her travels around the world, Donna creates pieces that evoke both tranquility and energy through her distinctive brushwork and bold color choices.

Donna's work has been exhibited in galleries across North America and Europe. Her paintings are part of numerous private and public collections.

She currently works from her studio in [Location], where she also hosts occasional workshops for emerging artists.
